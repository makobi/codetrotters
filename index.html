Gain Access to Talent
The Codetrotters program runs twice a year as an internship exchange, coinciding with universities’ winter and summer breaks. However, you may choose the exact duration of the internship based on your specific needs.
We do also work with companies that have immediate talent needs on a case-by-case basis.
Participating Cost
Our fellows are eager to gain invaluable global experiences. Although paying interns is optional, we do expect you to cover our fellows’ travel and living expenses estimated at approximately $3,000 per intern for a two-month period.
Choosing Talent
Absolutely. We do all the initial interviews and screening and then match the top coders with your particular needs. We will always give you more than one option and you have the final say on bringing them onboard your team.
Hiring
Of course! We encourage you to hire our Codetrotter fellows and facilitate that process. Many of our Codetrotters are still completing their degrees, but can be available on a part-time basis until graduation.
If you do decide to hire a Codetrotter, you will be charged a nominal fee that goes towards the sustainability of the program. (Only 50% of what regular recruiters charge).
Mentoring Hours
Participating startups are required to give back a minimum of 8 hours of mentoring to startups in underdeveloped ecosystems. We suggest that you physically send at least one person from your team to the city from which your fellow was sourced and host open office hours for startups in the community.
We will work with you to make the arrangements and ensure that you also take advantage of potential business development opportunities.
